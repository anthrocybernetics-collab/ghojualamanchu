## What It Does

The rcomplex is the body's environmental awareness. It knows where the system is, what the conditions are, and whether the environment is safe.

1. **Territory** — logs current location and environment
2. **Ritual** — tracks daily rhythms: dawn, midday, dusk, night
3. **Security** — scans for anomalies and raises threat level

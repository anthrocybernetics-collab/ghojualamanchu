# Amygdala — Brain Structure #3

**Role:** Emotion tagging, salience, threat/reward weighting
**Class:** Archetypal Limbic
**Position:** Midbrain (analogue)

---

## What It Does

The amygdala is the system's emotional engine. It *weights* every input: important or not, threat or reward, approach or avoid.

1. **Salience tagging** — every input gets scored 0-10
2. **Threat/reward weighting** — classifies inputs
3. **Emotional tone** — produces one-phrase emotional state

`memory/amygdala-salience.json` — recent events, threat/reward history, emotional trending

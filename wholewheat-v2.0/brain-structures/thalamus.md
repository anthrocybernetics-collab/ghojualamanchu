## What It Does

The thalamus is the central nervous system. Every input flows through here first, then gets distributed to all other brains simultaneously.

It does not process; it routes.

1. **Input reception** — receives from RSS, webhooks, triggers, user messages
2. **Parallel broadcast** — sends to all 8 brains at once
3. **Cycle coordination** — determines when cycles trigger
